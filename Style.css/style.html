Rose is an evergreen, shrubby, flowering plant. It has more than 100 species, most of which are of Asian origin. While some species are also native to Europe, North America and North West Africa. Rose is very popular among lovers because of its fragrance and beauty.

natures. uncountable noun Nature is all the animals, plants, and other things in the world that are not made by people, and all the events and processes that are not caused by people . The most amazing thing about nature is its infinite variety.
